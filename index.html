ONGOING INVESTIGATION DEC 8, 2024

Series of Explosions Target Pizzabakkers Chain—Third Suspect Arrested

A wave of explosions has targeted multiple locations of the Pizzabakkers restaurant chain in Amsterdam since late August. Four locations were temporarily closed by the deputy mayor after explosions at Plantage Kerklaan (two locations) and Overtoom. A 24-year-old Amsterdam man was arrested as the third suspect. The motive remains unclear, and the restaurant owners fear bankruptcy due to the closures.

NATIONAL POLITICS DEC 2, 2024

D66 and CDA Present Coalition Framework—Major Impact on Amsterdam

Political parties D66 and CDA have presented a 17-page coalition framework that will significantly impact Amsterdam. Key proposals include restoring the 30% expat tax ruling, building 100,000 homes annually, maintaining the asylum distribution law, and stricter migration measures. The framework represents an "extended hand" to other parties for potential coalition participation.
